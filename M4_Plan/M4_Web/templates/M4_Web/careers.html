Careers FAQs
What is the typical profile of the candidates we hire?

We look for young, creative, and motivated individuals and lay great emphasis on their educational qualifications. Further, the quality of prior experience, in relevant sectors, is essential for all candidates, except in the case of fresh graduates. We always look to having a good mix of people from various backgrounds, both in terms of education and experience, so as to widen our knowledge pool.
What is the usual career path for a Technopak employee?

A fresh graduate (with any professional degree and/or less than approximately two years of experience) will start as an Associate Consultant. Career growth is linked to performance, and a fast-track career is assured if the employee's performance is continually excellent.

What is the reporting structure prevalent within the organization?

We believe in teamwork and while, for administrative purposes, we have various hierarchical levels within the organization, viz. Senior Vice President, Vice President, Associate Vice President, Associate Director, Principal Consultant, Senior Consultant, Consultant, and Associate Consultant. Every employee has to report to the manager in charge of the project on which he/she is working. Occasionally, an individual may have to work on more than one project at the same time.
Do we hire summer trainees?

We do hire summer trainees as we believe this to be a good way of interacting with management schools. This provides students with a deep insight into our line of work, with projects ranging from market research and supply chain studies, to those based on our apparel operations. While we prefer to visit campuses for hiring summer trainees, we ask interested students, especially those from schools outside Delhi/NCR to send in their resumes. Subsequent to this, we interview shortlisted candidates. In case you are interested in doing a summer project with us next year, please email your resume to careers@technopak.com
What kind of projects can I expect to work on?

Although individuals, especially at the Associate Consultant or Consultant level, typically work on projects relevant to their background, there are ample opportunities to work on other areas as well. Consultants are consciously staffed on projects in a manner which not only allows them to work with cross-functional teams but also gives them a chance to work on projects in the firm's various service areas. Those employed at the Associate Director level and above have sales targets with respect to their divisions and are also expected to cross-sell within the service areas.
What is the level of focus on training within the organization?

There is tremendous emphasis on learning and knowledge-sharing within the organization. Besides the training programs coordinated by the HR division, training is also imparted while working on projects, and conducting client interactions. We believe that such hands-on experience successfully brings out the best qualities in people.
What is a typical day at Technopak?

A workday at Technopak begins at 8:30 a.m. and ends at 5.30 p.m., which ensures that our employees get in and out ahead of rush hour traffic. We observe a five-day work week, with the expectation that all business will be taken care of in this duration. Only unusual delivery timelines may see you spending the odd Saturday at work.
What kind of work culture can one expect at Technopak?

The Technopak culture encourages team effort but places equal stress on an individual’s development. We are, for the most part, dynamic, young people who cherish individual perceptions, and inspire top-notch performance.
